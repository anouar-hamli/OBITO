Obito Uchiha's Philosophy

Pain is the price of reality

Obito believes that pain and suffering are inseparable parts of life. To truly understand reality, one must face hardships. Escaping pain means being unable to confront the harsh truths of the world.

The ideal world can only be built through dreams

Obito dreamed of a world without sorrow and suffering. He saw reality as full of pain and believed that the only way to achieve true peace was to create a new world, implementing the "Eye of the Moon Plan" to impose an ideal dream on humanity.

True friendship never dies

Despite turning into Tobi and committing many wrongs, Obito's heart still longed for his bonds with Kakashi and Rin. In the end, he helped his friends and sacrificed himself for them, proving that genuine human connections endure.

Power without purpose is meaningless

Obito realized that strength alone is not enough. Purpose and vision give power its true meaning. This belief drove him to execute his plan in pursuit of what he saw as an ideal world.
